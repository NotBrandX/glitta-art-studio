About

Lita Lynch

From an early age, Lita Lynch showed great passion for the arts. She would experiment with interior design in her home and she would draw portraits of her siblings. Her dreams were shattered by the relatives who raised her, as they believed that artists were junkies who could not provide a good living.

Born and raised on the small island of Trinidad in the West Indies, Lita was raised with her aunt who was a dressmaker. From a young age, she was allowed to look through the fashion books and choose fabrics. She learned a lot from the professionals and clients who came in and out of the shop.

She completed high school and emigrated to the Unites States where she pursued a degree in Architecture, but she took a break to concentrate on her health and her family. During that time she started her own business as a Building Consultant as it provided the flexible hours she needed to make her family a priority. When her children were grown she returned to school to study her passion: art.

She gravitates towards landscape paintings with a twist of modern and abstract. Her chosen medium is oil. “I get most of my inspiration from the sky. I love to look up. I love to combine nature in my pieces.”
